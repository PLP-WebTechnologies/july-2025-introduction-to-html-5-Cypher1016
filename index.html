What is Web Accessibility?

Web accessibility means that websites, tools, and technologies are designed and developed so that people with disabilities can use them.

Key Principles (POUR)

  • Perceivable: Information must be presentable to users in ways they can perceive
  • Operable: Users must be able to operate the interface
  • Understandable: Information and operation must be understandable
  • Robust: Content must be robust enough to be interpreted reliably

SEO Basics

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the practice of increasing the quantity and quality of traffic to your website through organic search engine results.

On-Page SEO Factors

  1. Quality content
  2. Keyword optimization
  3. Meta tags
  4. Heading structure
  5. Image alt text

Semantic HTML5 Elements

Semantic HTML introduces meaning to the web page rather than just presentation.

Did You Know?

Over 1 billion people live with some form of disability. That's about 15% of the world's population.
